Vegetable Soup

Vegetable soup is a comforting and nourishing dish that showcases the vibrant flavors and colors of various vegetables. Packed with wholesome ingredients, this soup is a versatile and customizable option for any season. It typically features a medley of vegetables such as carrots, celery, onions, potatoes, tomatoes, and green beans, simmered in a flavorful broth. The vegetables release their natural flavors, creating a harmonious blend of tastes and textures. Whether enjoyed as a light and refreshing starter or a hearty main course, vegetable soup offers a comforting warmth and a satisfying way to incorporate a variety of nutritious vegetables into your meal. Its simplicity and adaptability make it a go-to choice for those seeking a healthy and delicious option that can be easily customized to suit individual preferences and dietary needs.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

Servings

6 servings

Tags: Carrots

Instructions

Step 1

Prepare the vegetables by washing them thoroughly. Peel and chop the carrots, celery, onion, potatoes, tomatoes, and any other vegetables you're using. Cut them into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2

Heat some oil in a large soup pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

Step 3

Add the chopped onions to the pot and cook for 2-3 minutes until they become translucent.

Step 4

Add the remaining vegetables (carrots, celery, potatoes, green beans, etc.) to the pot and stir well to combine.

Step 5

Pour in the vegetable broth or stock, enough to cover the vegetables. Adjust the quantity based on how thick or thin you prefer your soup.

Step 6

Add the bay leaves, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper to season the soup. Stir to incorporate the seasonings.

Step 7

Bring the soup to a boil over medium-high heat. Once it boils, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a lid, and let it simmer for about 20-30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.

Step 8

Taste the soup and adjust the seasonings if needed, adding more salt or pepper to suit your taste.

Step 9

Once the vegetables are cooked to your desired tenderness, remove the bay leaves from the soup.

Step 10

Serve the vegetable soup hot in bowls. Garnish with fresh herbs like parsley or basil, if desired.

Ingredients

Carrots (2-3 medium-sized)

Celery stalks (2-3)

Onion (1 medium-sized)

Potatoes (2 medium-sized)

Tomatoes (2 medium-sized) or canned diced tomatoes (14 oz/400g)

Green beans (1 cup)

Any other vegetables of your choice, such as zucchini, bell peppers, or peas (optional)

Broth: Begetable broth or stock (4-6 cups)

Garlic cloves (2-3, minced)

Bay leaves (2)

Dried thyme (1 teaspoon)

Salt (to taste)

Black pepper (to taste)

Olive oil or cooking oil for sautéing.

Tools

Cutting Board

Chef's Knife

Large Soup Pot or Dutch Oven

Wooden Spoon or Ladle

Vegetable Peeler

Can Opener

Measuring Cups and Spoons

Timer

Soup Bowls and Spoons
