Banana Pancakes

Banana pancakes are a delightful twist on traditional pancakes, infused with the sweet and creamy flavor of ripe bananas. The batter, made by mashing ripe bananas with flour, eggs, milk, and a touch of sugar and vanilla extract, creates fluffy and moist pancakes with a subtle banana taste. Served with maple syrup or whipped cream, these pancakes offer a comforting and indulgent treat. The natural sweetness of the bananas adds a delightful element, while the light and airy texture make each bite a delightful combination of fluffy pancake and gentle banana flavor. Whether enjoyed as is or customized with additions like chocolate chips or nuts, banana pancakes are a deliciously satisfying way to start the day on a sweet note.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

Servings

6 servings

Tags: Breakfast

Instructions

Step 1

In a mixing bowl, mash ripe bananas with a fork until smooth and creamy.

Step 2

Add flour, baking powder, sugar, salt, and cinnamon (if desired) to the mashed bananas. Stir until well combined.

Step 3

In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.

Step 4

Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the banana mixture. Stir until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined, but be careful not to overmix. A few lumps in the batter are okay.

Step 5

Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with butter or cooking spray.

Step 6

Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. If desired, you can make them smaller or larger based on your preference.

Step 7

Cook the pancakes for about 2-3 minutes on one side, or until bubbles start to form on the surface.

Step 8

Carefully flip the pancakes using a spatula and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until the other side is golden brown.

Step 9

Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate and cover with a clean kitchen towel to keep them warm while you cook the remaining batter.

Step 10

Serve the banana pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as sliced bananas, berries, maple syrup, honey, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk
